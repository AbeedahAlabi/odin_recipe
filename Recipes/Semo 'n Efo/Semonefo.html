How to prepare Nigerian Semovita and Vegetable soup

By - Abeedah Alabi

Vegetable soup with Semovita is one of the most popular of current trending meals in the world. It is easy, it's quick, it tastes yummy. It's enjoyed by millions every day. They are fine and they look wonderful. Vegetable soup with Semovita is something that I have loved my entire life.

To get started with this particular recipe, we have to first prepare a few ingredients. You can have vegetable soup and semo using 10 ingredients and 6 steps.

Ingredients

  1. Make ready Beef
  2. Make ready Semo(already prepared)
  3. Prepare Ugwu(sliced)
  4. Get Water leaf(sliced)
  5. Prepare Salt
  6. Take Crayfish
  7. Make ready Dry pepper
  8. Take Onions
  9. Get Palm oil
  10. Take Knorr seasoning cube

Steps

  1. Wash ur beef and place in a pot,add onions,knorr,salt and boil,add more water to cook till done.
  2. While d beef is still cooking,get ur leaves and wash them properly and set aside.
  3. Once d beef is done,add ur crayfish,onions,dry pepper and palm oil and simmer on high heat for a few mins till d stock has reduced and a bit thick.
  4. Add ur ugwu,cover and simmer for 2-3 mins.
  5. Open and add ur water leaf,stir thoroughly and simmer for 2-3 mins, taste for salt and correct accordingly.
  6. Ur veggie soup is ready and u can serve it with semo or any swallow of choice or rice and or yam

Go on get cooking!
